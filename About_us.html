Who We Are

A movement built by Malawians, for Malawians—determined to unlock the genius in every young person.

Meet the team, our story, and the values that drive us.

Our Mission

To discover hidden talents across Malawi, equip them with one mastery, and connect them directly to industry—creating a new generation of job creators and industrialists.

Our Vision

A Malawi where every young person’s skill, not their degree, determines their opportunity; where the Mastery Economy replaces the paper ceiling, and where our nation becomes a hub of innovation and industry.

How It Began

The idea for Mastery Ecosystem was born not in a boardroom, but in a village in Mzimba. Our founder, Chimwembe Banda, watched a young man repair a diesel engine with scraps and ingenuity—yet he was labelled ‘unskilled’ because he had no formal qualifications. At the same time, companies in Lilongwe were desperate for technicians. That gap—between raw talent and economic opportunity—became our calling. We started with one radio announcement and a handful of youth under a tree. Today, we’ve grown to three hubs, over 150 graduates, and partnerships with companies across the country. But the principle remains the same: talent is universal; opportunity should be too.

“Talent is universal; opportunity should be too.”
